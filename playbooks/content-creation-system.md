# 🎨 Content Creation System

**Goal:** Turn your ideas into finished posts, videos, or newsletters without getting stuck.

* **Tools Used:** ChatGPT or Claude (for writing and ideas), Notion (for keeping track of your schedule), and Canva or CapCut (for making pictures and video).

* **Expected Output:** A finished piece of content that is ready to post online today.

## ⏱️ The 2-Hour Creation Block

* **[15 mins] Find the Idea:** Open ChatGPT or Claude. Ask it to give you 10 fresh ideas for a post about your topic. Pick the best one.

* **[45 mins] The Rough Draft:** Ask the AI to write a messy first draft, an outline, or a video script based on the idea you chose. Copy it into your Notion notebook.

* **[45 mins] Make It Yours:** Do not just copy and paste the AI's work! Read through the draft. Change the words so they sound like you, add your own real-life stories, and make a nice picture in Canva if you need one.

* **[15 mins] Schedule It:** Put your finished post onto your blog or social media accounts, and set the time for it to go live.
