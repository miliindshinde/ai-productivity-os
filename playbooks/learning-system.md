# 📚 The Learning System

**Goal:** Understand new, hard topics quickly and actually remember what you learned.

* **Tools Used:** NotebookLM (for talking to your notes) or ChatGPT, and Notion (for saving notes).
* **Expected Output:** A clear, simple summary of a difficult topic and a set of notes you can review later.

## ⏱️ The 1-Hour Study Block

* **[15 mins] Gather the Material:** Find the long article, textbook chapter, or YouTube video you need to learn. Copy the text or the transcript and save it in your Notion notebook.

* **[30 mins] Your AI Tutor:** Upload that text into NotebookLM or ChatGPT. Do not just read the text yourself. Instead, ask the AI: "Explain the top 5 main ideas in this text like I am a beginner." Read what it gives you.

* **[15 mins] Test Yourself:** Ask the AI to write 3 short quiz questions about the topic to see if you actually learned it. Answer them. Then, copy the AI's simple summary and paste it into your Notion notebook so you never have to read the long version again.
