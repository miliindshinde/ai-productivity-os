# 👔 Daily AI Workflow for Professionals

**Goal:** Get through your daily emails and busywork much faster so you can focus on real work.

* **Tools Used:** Google Gemini (or ChatGPT), Notion, and Make.com.
* **Expected Output:** An empty email inbox, drafted replies, and a clear plan for the day.

## ⏱️ The 1-Hour Morning Routine

* **[15 mins] The Sweep:** Open your email. Let your Make.com robot automatically save any emails with the label "Important" straight into your Notion notebook. Delete the junk.
  
* **[30 mins] AI Replies:** Pick the 3 hardest emails you need to answer. Open Gemini and ask it to write the replies for you. (Example: "Write a polite email telling my client their project will be two days late, but offer them a small discount.") Read them, fix any mistakes, and send.

* **[15 mins] Daily Plan:** Open Notion. Look at the important emails your robot saved. Write down the top 3 things you absolutely must finish today based on those emails.
