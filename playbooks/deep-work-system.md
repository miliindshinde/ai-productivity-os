# 🧠 Focused Deep Work System

**Goal:** Block out distractions and get hard work done without losing focus.

* **Tools Used:** Perplexity (for fast research), Notion (for writing), and Apple Shortcuts or Make.com (for blocking apps).
* **Expected Output:** A finished piece of hard work (like a report, essay, or code) and zero distractions.

## ⏱️ The 2-Hour Focus Block

* **[15 mins] The Setup:** Use an automation tool or your phone's settings to block all social media and text messages for the next two hours. Open a blank page in Notion.
* **[30 mins] Fast Research:** Open Perplexity. Ask it to find all the facts, links, and numbers you need for your project. Copy the best notes into your Notion page.
* **[60 mins] Deep Work:** Close all extra browser tabs. Look at your notes and start writing, designing, or coding. Do not stop or check email until the hour is up.
* **[15 mins] The Wrap-Up:** Save your work. Turn your messages back on. Write down exactly what you need to do next time so you can start easily tomorrow.
