# 📋 Prompt Templates

A library of ready-to-use prompts for the most common daily tasks. Copy, paste, and fill in the parts in `[brackets]`.

---

## How to Use This Library

1. Pick the template that matches your task
2. Copy it into your AI tool (Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT, etc.)
3. Replace the `[bracketed text]` with your specific details
4. Hit send

Save the ones you use often in your Notion workspace so they are always at hand.

---

## ✉️ Email & Communication

### Write a Professional Email
```
Write a professional email to [recipient's role, e.g. "a potential client"].

The purpose of the email is to [goal, e.g. "introduce my freelance design services"].

Key points to include:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

Tone: [e.g. "friendly but professional"]
Length: Keep it under 150 words.
```

### Follow-Up Email
```
Write a short, polite follow-up email to [recipient] about [topic].

Context: I sent them an email [timeframe, e.g. "one week ago"] but haven't heard back.
My goal is to [desired outcome, e.g. "schedule a 15-minute call"].

Keep it under 80 words and avoid sounding pushy.
```

### Summarize a Long Email Thread
```
Here is an email thread. Please summarize it in 3 bullet points:
1. What is the main issue or topic?
2. What decisions or action items were agreed on?
3. What is still unresolved or needs a response?

[Paste the email thread here]
```

## 📄 Writing & Editing

### First Draft of Any Document
```
Write a first draft of a [document type, e.g. "blog post / proposal / report"] about [topic].

Audience: [who will read this]
Tone: [e.g. "casual", "formal", "encouraging"]
Length: approximately [word count or length]
Key points to cover:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]
```

### Edit and Improve My Writing
```
Please improve the following text. Keep my original meaning and voice, but:
- Fix any grammar or spelling mistakes
- Make the sentences clearer and easier to read
- Remove any unnecessary words

[Paste your text here]
```

### Make It Shorter
```
Rewrite the following text to be about half the length.
Keep the most important information. Remove filler words and repetition.

[Paste your text here]
```

## 🧠 Research & Learning

### Explain a Complex Topic Simply
```
Explain [topic] as if I am a complete beginner with no background in the subject.
Use a simple analogy to help me understand it.
Keep your answer to 3 short paragraphs.
```

### Pros and Cons Analysis
```
Give me a balanced pros and cons list for [decision or topic].

Context: [brief description of your situation]

Format it as two clear lists: Pros and Cons.
End with a one-sentence summary of the overall trade-off.
```

### Research Summary
```
Summarize what you know about [topic].
Focus on:
1. The core idea in one sentence
2. The 3 most important facts or findings
3. Any common misconceptions
4. Where I should look to learn more

Keep it under 200 words.
```

## 📱 Content Creation

### Social Media Post
```
Write [number] social media posts about [topic] for [platform, e.g. LinkedIn / Instagram / X].

Tone: [e.g. "professional", "casual", "inspiring"]
Goal: [e.g. "drive engagement", "share a tip", "announce something"]
Include a call-to-action at the end of each post.
Do NOT use hashtags unless I ask.
```

### Video Script Outline
```
Write a short video script outline for a [length, e.g. "60-second"] video about [topic].

Format:
- Hook (first 5 seconds): grab attention immediately
- Main Point 1
- Main Point 2
- Main Point 3
- Call to action (last 5 seconds)

Tone: [e.g. "energetic", "calm and informative"]
```

### Newsletter Section
```
Write one section for my weekly newsletter about [topic].

My newsletter is for [audience description].
The tone is [tone].
The section should be about [100–150] words and end with one actionable takeaway.
```

## 💼 Work & Productivity

### Meeting Agenda
```
Create a meeting agenda for a [length, e.g. "30-minute"] meeting about [purpose].

Attendees: [list roles, not names, e.g. "team lead, designer, developer"]
Goal of the meeting: [what you want to decide or accomplish]

Format it with time slots for each agenda item.
```

### Summarize Meeting Notes
```
Here are my raw notes from a meeting. Please:
1. Write a 3-sentence summary of what was discussed
2. List all action items with the responsible person noted
3. List any unresolved questions

[Paste your raw notes here]
```

### Weekly Review Prompt
```
Help me do a quick weekly review. I will answer three questions and you help me synthesize them into a plan for next week.

1. What went well this week? [Your answer]
2. What did not go as planned? [Your answer]
3. What are my top 3 priorities for next week? [Your answer]

Based on my answers, give me: one insight, one thing to stop or change, and a clear priority order for next week.
```

## 🎓 Studying & Learning

### Study Guide From Notes
```
Here are my notes on [topic]. Turn them into a concise study guide with:
- A brief overview (2-3 sentences)
- Key terms and their definitions
- The 5 most important concepts to remember
- 3 practice questions to test my understanding

[Paste your notes here]
```

### Explain Why I Got Something Wrong
```
I answered this question wrong:

Question: [the question]
My answer: [what you said]
Correct answer: [the right answer]

Please explain why my answer was wrong and help me understand the correct reasoning clearly.
```

## 💡 Brainstorming

### Generate Ideas
```
Give me [number] ideas for [topic or goal].

Context: [brief description of your situation, constraints, or audience]
Make the ideas practical and specific, not vague.
```

### Challenge My Idea
```
Here is an idea I am considering: [your idea]

Please give me:
1. The strongest argument FOR this idea
2. The strongest argument AGAINST this idea
3. One thing I might be overlooking entirely
```
