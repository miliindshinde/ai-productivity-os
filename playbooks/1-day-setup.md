# ⏳ The 1-Day Setup Guide

You can build your complete AI system in just one afternoon. Follow this simple hour-by-hour guide to get your 3 main tools working together today.

## Hour 1: Create Your Accounts
**Goal:** Get the keys to your new tools.
* **15 mins:** Go to [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com) and sign in with your Google account. Say "Hello" to make sure it works.
* **15 mins:** Go to [Notion](https://www.notion.so) and create a free account. Make a new blank page and call it "My AI System".
* **30 mins:** Go to [Make.com](https://www.make.com) and create a free account. Click around just to see what the dashboard looks like.

## Hour 2: The Brain & The Memory
**Goal:** Make your AI do some work, and save the result.
* **20 mins:** Open Gemini. Ask it a real question, like: "Write a polite email to my boss asking for next Friday off."
* **20 mins:** Read the email Gemini wrote. If it needs changes, ask Gemini to fix it.
* **20 mins:** Copy the final email. Open your Notion "My AI System" page, paste the email there, and save it. You just connected your Brain to your Memory!

## Hour 3: The Hands (Your First Robot)
**Goal:** Make things happen automatically.
* **30 mins:** Open Make.com and click "Create a new scenario" (this just means a new automation). 
* **30 mins:** Follow Make's quick beginner tutorial to connect two simple apps. For example, try making a rule that says: "When I get a new email, send me a message on my phone."

**Congratulations!** You have just built a working AI system in 3 hours.
