# 🏛️ Poe: The Mega-Mall of AI Brains

### 1. Tool Overview

Poe (made by the people at Quora) is a platform that brings all the world’s best AI models together in one app. Instead of jumping between ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini websites, you can use them all inside Poe. In 2026, it is the best place to compare different AIs to see which one gives you the smartest answer.

### 2. Primary Use Cases

* **Model Comparing:** Ask the same question to three different AIs (like GPT-5, Claude 4, and Gemini 3) at the exact same time to see who is right.

* **Custom Bot Building:** You can create your own "mini-AI" in 60 seconds. For example, you can make a "History Tutor" that only explains things using 10th-grade language.

* **All-in-One Creativity:** Use Poe to write a story with one AI, then instantly switch to a different AI in the same chat to turn that story into a picture or a 3D video.

### 3. How to Learn (Your Day 1 Path)

* **Step 1 (The Test Drive):** Go to [poe.com](https://poe.com). Pick any bot from the sidebar (like "Assistant") and ask a question.

* **Step 2 (The Multi-Chat):** Start a "Multi-Bot Chat." Mention two different bots (like @ChatGPT and @Claude) and ask them to debate a topic.

* **Step 3 (Create Your Own):** Click "Create Bot." Give it a name like "My Homework Helper" and tell it: "Always explain things simply and end every answer with a joke." Save it and try it out!

### 4. Alternatives

* **ChatGPT:** Better if you only want to use OpenAI’s tools and want the most polished mobile app.

* **TypingMind:** A professional tool for people who have their own "API keys" and want more control over their data.

* **Hugging Chat:** The best free, open-source version where you can try models built by the community.

### 5. Key Differences vs Competitors

The biggest difference is **The Subscription.** Usually, if you want the best version of ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, you would have to pay $60 a month. With Poe, you pay one price and get access to the "Pro" versions of all of them. It also uses a system called **Compute Points**—some brains are "expensive" to use, and some are "cheap."

### 6. Benefits & Limitations

* **Benefits:** You get the newest AI models the same day they are released. It works on your phone, web, and computer.

* **Limitations:** The "Compute Points" system can be confusing—if you use the smartest AI too much, you might run out of points for the day.

### 7. Real-World Mini Case Study

**The Student Researcher:** A high school student was writing a big report on climate change. He noticed that ChatGPT gave him one set of facts, but Gemini gave him different ones. Using Poe’s **Multi-Bot Chat**, he put both AIs in the same room and asked them to "Double-check each other’s sources." They caught each other’s mistakes, and he ended up with a much more accurate report than if he had used just one tool.

### 8. When to Choose This Tool

Choose Poe if you are an "AI Explorer" who wants to try everything. It is the perfect choice for anyone who wants the most powerful AI brains without spending a fortune on multiple subscriptions.

### 9. Difficulty Level

**Beginner.** It is very simple to use, but learning how to manage your "Compute Points" takes a little bit of practice.

### 10. Pricing Model (2026)

* **Free:** Access to many models with a daily limit of compute points.

* **Basic ($5/mo):** A newer plan that gives you more points than the free version but is cheaper than the full Pro plan.

* **Poe Plus ($19.99/mo):** 1 million compute points per month. This lets you use the world’s smartest models (like GPT-5 and Claude 4) every day.

* **Poe Pro ($200/mo):** For "Super Users" who need millions of points for heavy video and image work.
