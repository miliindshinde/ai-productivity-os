# 🗓️ 30-Day System Building Plan

The goal of this month is to go from "just trying out AI" to having a personalized machine that works for you every day.

## Week 1: The Core Foundation

* **Days 1-7:** Follow the [7-Day Quick Start Plan](./7-day-quick-start.md). Focus on getting your first 3 tools (Gemini, Notion, Make.com) working together.

## Week 2: Expanding Your Skills

* **Days 8-12:** Try a different playbook every day (Deep Work, Learning, or Content Creation). See which one feels most natural for your work.

* **Days 13-14:** Explore the [Master Tool List](../tools/tool-list.md). Choose two new tools to try out (like Canva for design or Perplexity for research).

## Week 3: Smart Automations

* **Days 15-21:** This is "Automation Week." Every day, try to build one small automation in Make.com or Zapier.

    * *Example:* "Save my favorite tweets to a Notion list."
  
    * *Example:* "Send a daily summary of the weather to my email."

## Week 4: Custom Mastery

* **Days 22-27:** Choose your favorite [Core Stack](../stacks/core-stacks.md) (Student, Freelancer, or Creator) and use only those tools for all your big projects this week.

* **Days 28-30:** Review your Notion system. Delete any pages you didn't use and clean up your notes. You now have a custom AI Productivity OS!
