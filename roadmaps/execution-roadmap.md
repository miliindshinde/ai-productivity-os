# 🗺️ APOS Execution Roadmaps

This is not about theory; it is about taking action. Follow these roadmaps to build your system step-by-step.

## 🟢 The 7-Day Foundation (Beginner)
**Goal:** Set up your basic system and build a daily AI habit.
* **Day 1:** Create your accounts (Gemini/ChatGPT, Notion, Make.com).
* **Day 2:** Build your "Command Center" dashboard in Notion.
* **Day 3:** Write and save your first 3 AI prompts in your notes.
* **Day 4:** Use AI to summarize a 1-hour video or long article into bullet points.
* **Day 5:** Complete the beginner tutorial on Make.com.
* **Day 6:** Build a simple automation (like saving starred emails to Notion).
* **Day 7:** Complete your first Weekly Reflection.

## 🔵 The 30-Day Automation Engine (Intermediate)
**Goal:** Get 5+ hours a week back by automating boring digital chores.
* **Days 8-14:** Automate how you save information. Send articles and emails straight to a reading list.
* **Days 15-21:** Connect AI to your automation. (Example: When a new article is saved, AI automatically writes a 3-sentence summary).
* **Days 22-28:** Create a workflow that turns your rough notes into finished emails or posts. 
* **Days 29-30:** Review your system. What broke? What takes too long? Fix it.
