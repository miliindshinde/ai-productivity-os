# 🚀 7-Day Quick Start Plan

The goal of this week is to get your "3-Tool System" (Gemini, Notion, and Make.com) working together.

### Day 1: The Setup
* **Task:** Create accounts for Gemini, Notion, and Make.com.

* **Output:** Three working tools ready for action.

### Day 2: Your First AI Draft
* **Task:** Use Gemini to write a difficult email or a short project plan.

* **Output:** Copy the best parts into a new Notion page called "Daily Wins."

### Day 3: Simple Automation
* **Task:** Follow a tutorial on Make.com to connect your email to your Notion page.

* **Output:** One working "robot" that saves information for you.

### Day 4: Professional Workflow
* **Task:** Follow the [Professional Workflow Playbook](../playbooks/professional-workflow.md) for 1 hour.

* **Output:** An empty inbox and a clear plan for your day.

### Day 5: Deep Work
* **Task:** Pick one hard task and follow the [Deep Work System](../playbooks/deep-work-system.md).

* **Output:** One finished project and zero distractions for 2 hours.

### Day 6: Expanding the System
* **Task:** Pick one new tool from the [Master Tool List](../tools/tool-list.md) and try it out.

* **Output:** A fourth tool added to your personal toolkit.

### Day 7: Weekly Review
* **Task:** Look at your Notion "Daily Wins" page. Write down one thing that worked well this week and one thing to improve next week.

* **Output:** A clear mindset for starting Week 2.
