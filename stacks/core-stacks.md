# 🧱 The Core Stacks

A "stack" is just a group of tools that work well together. You do not need every tool on the master list. Just pick the stack that matches what you do.

## 🎓 The Student Stack
**Goal:** Learn faster, summarize research, and never miss a due date.
* **Brain:** Google Gemini (great for explaining hard topics simply).
* **Memory:** Notion (for keeping class notes and tracking assignments).
* **Hands:** Make.com (to automatically save important school emails directly into Notion).

## 💼 The Freelancer Stack
**Goal:** Spend less time on boring paperwork and more time doing the actual job.
* **Brain:** Claude (best for writing polite, professional emails to clients).
* **Memory:** Airtable (for tracking who paid you and what projects are due).
* **Hands:** Zapier (to automatically send a welcome email when a new client fills out a form).

## 🎨 The Creator Stack
**Goal:** Come up with ideas, make videos or posts, and schedule them easily.
* **Brain:** ChatGPT (great for brainstorming wild ideas and writing video scripts).
* **Memory:** Apple Notes or Google Keep (for saving quick ideas on your phone while you are out).
* **Hands:** Make.com (to automatically move a finished script into your schedule).
